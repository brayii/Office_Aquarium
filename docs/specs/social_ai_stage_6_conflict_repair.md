# Social Personality AI Stage 6: Conflict and Repair

Stage 6 turns real negative interactions into persistent professional conflict and allows later source-backed actions to repair it. Conflict is not random office drama and is not a direct project penalty.

## Sources

Conflict can begin from concrete events such as:

- professional disagreement
- ignored request
- interruption
- credit dispute
- deadline blame
- failed collaboration
- dismissive comment
- meeting conflict

Every conflict retains its source event, participants, context, personality interpretation, emotional recommendation, and relationship state before and after the event.

## Repair

Repair requires a real unresolved conflict and a later action such as:

- apology
- clarification
- constructive follow-up
- constructive feedback
- accepted recognition
- successful repair

Acceptance is influenced by forgiveness, trust, conflict intensity, elapsed time, and evidence-backed leadership credibility. A generic helpful action does not erase conflict unless it is actually linked to that conflict. Failed and accepted attempts remain in history.

## Boundary

Conflict and repair may:

- affect hidden relationship interpretation
- create bounded stress or morale recommendations
- influence future voluntary social preference
- contribute evidence to culture, groups, and leadership

They may not directly modify work, project progress, quality, productivity, hiring, customers, or Institutional Learning.
