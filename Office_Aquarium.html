Player Guide

How to Read Office Aquarium

You are the CEO

Employees choose their own work, ask for help, learn from outcomes, and escalate only the decisions that need executive authority.

Watch the office

The Live Office shows what people are doing now. Quiet moments usually mean teams are handling work without needing you.

Use the CEO Inbox

Email and memos are where you make decisions. Read the situation, compare the choices, and remember that advice can be imperfect.

Read Company views

Overview is the daily dashboard. Projects shows portfolio pressure. Workforce shows hiring and staffing. Story shows history and consequences.

Lead indirectly
  • Set priorities through decisions.
  • Approve or delay headcount.
  • Protect quality, cash, people, or speed.
  • Let departments execute the details.
Success is balance

Cash matters, but so do trust, product health, customers, morale, board confidence, and the company's ability to recover from mistakes.
